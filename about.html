Eileen Bayagau

Is a Papua New Guinean artist who has a passion for sharing her creativity and talent with others. She is a long time Art Teacher and a sought after artist. Her work is regularly on display at the Ella Beach market. Ebay, amongst other things, is a talented painter. She is also skilled in printmaking, drawing, design and handicrafts. Ebay is the creative partner in Canvas Corner. She designs all of the artwork, trains new artists, and is the main teacher for our our classes. Just browse through the image gallery to see some of her work.


Jane Moore

Jane is Australian expat living in Papua New Guinea. She and her family have been in PNG for a number of years. Sadly, possessing very little artistic talent of her own, she loves the opportunity to paint and draw that the Canvas Corner classes provide. Jane manages the logistics – purchases, materials, provides a space for the art and provides a fridge for the wine! If you have any questions – fire them her way – just click on the Email Us button on our Facebook page or visit the Contact Us page.
